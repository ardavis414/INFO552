International Center for MultiGenerational Legacies of Trauma

Data Wrangling and Cataloging Intern

In compliance with the Master's in Library and Information Science degree requirements at Drexel, I am currently working in collaboration with the International Center for MultiGenerational Legacies of Trauma (ICMGLT) as an intern for course credit. The ICMGLT is a non-profit organization that is dedicated to:

  • raising awareness of ongoing suffering by identity groups that have been exposed to multigenerational legacies of trauma.
  • preventing future generations of people from inheriting the same trauma from their parents and grandparents.
  • advocating for societal awareness of the harms of generational trauma andâ€‚providing educational resources to combat it.

As a data wrangling and metadata intern, I am supporting ICMGLT's vision for migrating its library records from its current database host, Wordpress, to another repository that specializes in long-term data preservation and stewardship. My role as an intern is to use OpenRefine to clean up and refine hundreds of the ICMGLT library's records in preparation for the future migration.
